# Moya Rituals Landing Page Project Summary

## 1. Project Goal & Intent

The primary goal of this project is to create a lean validation MVP landing page for a new skincare brand, **Moya Rituals**. The core hypothesis to be tested is: "People are interested in neuro-calming skincare."

The key success metric is the **email signup conversion rate**, with a target of 5-10%. The project follows "The Lean Startup" methodology, using a waitlist to gauge product-market fit before building out full e-commerce functionality.

## 2. Product: The Ritual System

Moya Rituals offers a simple, two-product system designed to be a daily "ritual to reset your nervous system."

- **Product 1: MindSerum™**
  - **Type**: Neuro-Calming Serum
  - **Description**: A lightweight serum with adaptogens and neuro-calming botanicals to soothe stressed skin.
  - **Key Benefits**: Reduces signs of stress, supports the skin barrier, calms redness.

- **Product 2: Ritual Mist™**
  - **Type**: Aromatic Reset Mist
  - **Description**: A facial mist to ground the senses and prepare the skin for the serum.
  - **Key Benefits**: Instantly hydrates, provides a grounding aromatic experience.

- **The 3-Step Ritual**:
  1.  **Pause & Breathe**: A moment of mindfulness.
  2.  **Mist & Ground**: Apply the **Ritual Mist**.
  3.  **Smooth & Calm**: Apply the **MindSerum**.

## 3. Brand Identity & Messaging

- **Core Tagline**: "The skincare ritual to reset your nervous system."
- **Brand Statement**: "Calm Skin. Clear Head."
- **Key Themes**: Mindfulness, nervous system support, clean ingredients, simplicity (a 3-minute ritual).
